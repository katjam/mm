MANDI MARTIN

Singing Coach

Originally a student of the celebrated soprano, Gwen Catley, and latterly, Mary King, Mandi teaches with emphasis on a good classical technique, as a basis for different styles of singing. Her teaching career has run parallel with her career as a performer in musicals. West End credits include Me and My Girl, A Little Night Music, Winnie, Call Me Madam, Annie, Umbrellas of Cherbourg, Oliver and Bubbling Brown Sugar. Regional theatre work includes My Fair Lady, The Sound of Music, Mr Cinders and Divorce Me Darling at the Chichester Festival Theatre.
